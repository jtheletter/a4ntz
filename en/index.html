Platform of the International Alliance for Sustainable Local Time

Several associations for permanent natural time zones have joined to raise awareness among citizens of the importance of a correct time use.

A mounting number of scientific studies show that a life against the internal clock makes us sick. It increases the risk for cancer, metabolic diseases, cardiovascular problems, sleep disorders, depression and reduces life expectancy. This is detrimental on an individual level and on society at large, since it burdens the economy and the healthcare system and pollutes the environment.

One reason why people live against their internal clock is due to changing the clock twice a year. Another is countries choosing geographically incorrect time zones. This further decouples social time from solar time, the latter of which is the main regulator for our body clock.

We want to give people the right information about the advantages and disadvantages of choosing a time zone. We encourage politicians and governments to recognize the need to live in a time zone geographically appropriate for each country and to seek reliable data in order to decide.

It is a right to live in a system that respects our biorhythm. This will have a positive impact on heart attacks, diabetes, immunity, and prevent cancer. It will improve mental performance at school. Sleep quality will increase. During the summer months we can enjoy abundant sunlight. Living in the correct time zone ensures safer traffic, especially in spring and autumn. Time observation that naturally suits the time zone in which one is located is one of the solutions to the climate issue. It must be seen in the spirit of a sustainable society.

Our governments should not neglect the well-being of the people, especially the most vulnerable in society. We request the European Union, National Authorities, and all federal, state, and provincial governments, without delay, to publish concrete data in 2020 and take final decisions.

To each country its own natural time zone.
